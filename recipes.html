01 — Butter Chicken

Pakistani Butter Chicken

Instant Pot ~50 min Serves 4 + leftovers Kid-approved

Modeled on Cloud Naan (Long Beach). Debbie called it her favorite dish. The kasuri methi at the end is the move — don't skip it.

Ingredients

Chicken
  • 2 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs, bite-size
Marinade
  • 1 cup whole-milk yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp pre-minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp kashmiri chili
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
Sauce
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 yellow onion, fine dice
  • 1½ tsp pre-minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp kashmiri chili
Finish
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi
  • cilantro, to garnish
Serve with
  • jasmine rice + naan

Method

  1. Marinate (1–24 hrs ahead). Mix marinade, coat chicken, cover, refrigerate. Full day = best flavor.
  2. Sauté mode (~12 min). Melt 2 tbsp butter. Soften onion 8–10 min. Add garlic + ginger, 1 min. Stir in tomato paste + all sauce spices, 1 min.
  3. Assemble. Add crushed tomatoes + raw marinated chicken. Layer tomatoes on top — don't scrape the fond. Add ¼ cup water if dry. No cream yet. Seal.
  4. Pressure cook. HIGH 8 min → natural release 5 min → quick-release.
  5. Optional. Pull chicken, immersion-blend sauce smooth, return chicken.
  6. Finish. Stir in heavy cream + butter. Crush kasuri methi between palms, stir in off the heat. Simmer 2 min. Salt to taste. Garnish cilantro.
Cream + kasuri methi always after pressure cooking — dairy curdles under pressure. The kasuri methi off the heat is the Cloud Naan move; don't rush it.

02 — Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala

Grill + Instant Pot ~60 min Serves 4 + leftovers

Smokier and tangier than butter chicken. The char on the grill is what makes it tikka masala — don't skip that step.

Ingredients

Chicken
  • 2 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs, bite-size
Marinade
  • 1 cup whole-milk yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp pre-minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp kashmiri chili
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
Sauce
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 yellow onion, fine dice
  • 1½ tsp pre-minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp kashmiri chili
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp turmeric
Finish
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi
  • cilantro, to garnish
Serve with
  • jasmine rice + naan

Method

  1. Marinate (1–24 hrs ahead). Mix marinade, coat chicken, cover, refrigerate. Overnight = best.
  2. Measure sauce spices into a small bowl now — you'll dump the whole bowl in at once during the sauté step.
  3. Char the chicken. Grill (preferred): HIGH heat, foil or grill basket, 8–10 min, flip once. Want dark spots. Doesn't need to be cooked through — finishes in the pot. Broiler backup: HIGH, top rack, 8–10 min, flip halfway.
  4. Sauté mode (~12 min). Melt butter. Soften onion 8–10 min. Add garlic + ginger, 1 min. Stir in tomato paste + sauce spice bowl, 1 min.
  5. Assemble. Add crushed tomatoes + charred chicken + any resting juices from the grill. Layer tomatoes on top, ¼ cup water if dry. No cream yet. Seal.
  6. Pressure cook. HIGH 8 min → natural release 5 min → quick-release.
  7. Optional. Pull chicken, immersion-blend sauce smooth, return chicken.
  8. Finish. Stir in heavy cream + butter. Crush kasuri methi between palms, stir in off the heat. Simmer 2 min. Salt to taste. Garnish cilantro.
The smoked paprika does the work the charcoal grill does in a restaurant — don't omit it. If the sauce tastes flat after the IP, a splash of lemon juice or a pinch of sugar balances it.

03 — Mango Lassi

Mango Lassi

Blender 5 min Serves 4–5 Make ahead

Frozen + fresh mango gives you both body and fresh flavor. Make it first — it improves after 30 min in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups frozen mango chunks
  • 1 cup fresh mango, roughly chopped
  • 1½ cups whole-milk yogurt
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2–3 tbsp sugar
  • ½ tsp ground cardamom
  • pinch salt

Method

  1. Blend everything until completely smooth — frozen mango + yogurt + milk + sugar + cardamom + salt.
  2. Taste. Adjust sugar. If too thick, add milk a splash at a time.
  3. Pour into a pitcher, refrigerate until dinner. Serve cold.
Make this before you start cooking — it only gets better as it sits and everything melds together.
