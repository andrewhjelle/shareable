01 — Butter Chicken

Pakistani Butter Chicken

Instant Pot ~50 min active Serves 4 + leftovers Kid-approved

Modeled on Cloud Naan (Long Beach). Debbie called it her favorite dish. The kasuri methi off heat is the move — don’t skip it.

Tools
Instant Pot Large bowl (marinade) Small bowl (sauce spices) Cutting board + knife Grater (ginger) Measuring spoons + cups Immersion blender (optional)
Before you cook
  1. Measure marinade spices into yogurt bowl — dump all at once
  2. Measure sauce spices into a small bowl (already in the cabinet) — dump the whole bowl at step 2
  3. Dice onion, measure garlic, grate ginger — all at once on the cutting board
  4. Set finish tray aside: cream, 1 tbsp butter, kasuri methi

Ingredients

Marinade bowl Measure now
  • 2 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs, bite-size
  • 1 cup whole-milk yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp pre-minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp kashmiri chili
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
Sauce spice bowl Pre-measure
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp kashmiri chili
Saute During cook
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 yellow onion, fine dice
  • 1.5 tsp pre-minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
Finish tray Set aside
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi
  • — cilantro, to garnish
Serve with
  • — jasmine rice + naan

Method

  1. Marinate (1–24 hrs ahead). Coat chicken in marinade bowl, cover, refrigerate. Full day = best flavor.
  2. Saute mode (~12 min). Melt 2 tbsp butter. Soften onion 8–10 min. Add garlic + ginger, 1 min. Stir in tomato paste, then dump the sauce spice bowl in, 1 min.
  3. Assemble. Add crushed tomatoes + raw marinated chicken. Layer tomatoes on top — don’t scrape the fond. Add 1/4 cup water if dry. No cream yet. Seal.
  4. Pressure cook. HIGH 8 min → natural release 5 min → quick-release.
  5. Optional. Pull chicken, immersion-blend sauce smooth, return chicken.
  6. Finish (from finish tray). Stir in heavy cream + butter. Crush kasuri methi between palms, stir in off the heat. Simmer 2 min. Salt to taste. Garnish cilantro.
Cream + kasuri methi always after pressure cooking — dairy curdles under pressure. Kasuri methi off heat is the Cloud Naan move.

02 — Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala

Grill + Instant Pot ~60 min active Serves 4 + leftovers

Smokier and tangier than butter chicken. The char is what makes it tikka masala — don’t skip the grill step.

Tools
Instant Pot Grill + foil or basket Large bowl (marinade) Small bowl (sauce spices) Cutting board + knife Grater (ginger) Measuring spoons + cups Immersion blender (optional)
Before you cook
  1. Night before: marinade bowl + coat chicken. 5 minutes
  2. Measure sauce spices into a small bowl (dump it all at once during saute)
  3. Dice onion, grate ginger, measure garlic — all at once
  4. Set finish tray: cream, butter, kasuri methi, cilantro
  5. Fire grill to HIGH while onion softens in the IP — they finish at the same time

Ingredients

Marinade bowl Night before
  • 2 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs, bite-size
  • 1 cup whole-milk yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp pre-minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp kashmiri chili
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
Sauce spice bowl Pre-measure
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp kashmiri chili
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
Saute During cook
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 yellow onion, fine dice
  • 1.5 tsp pre-minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
Finish tray Set aside
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi
  • — cilantro, to garnish
Serve with
  • — jasmine rice + naan

Method

  1. Marinate (night before). Coat chicken in marinade bowl, cover, refrigerate.
  2. Saute mode (~12 min). Melt butter. Soften onion 8–10 min. Add garlic + ginger, 1 min. Stir in tomato paste + dump sauce spice bowl in, 1 min. While onion softens: fire grill to HIGH.
  3. Grill the chicken. Foil on grill or grill basket, HIGH heat, 8–10 min, flip once. Want dark spots. Doesn’t need to cook through — finishes in the pot. Broiler backup: HIGH, top rack, 8–10 min, flip halfway.
  4. Assemble. Add crushed tomatoes + charred chicken + any resting juices. Layer tomatoes on top, 1/4 cup water if dry. No cream yet. Seal.
  5. Pressure cook. HIGH 8 min → natural release 5 min → quick-release.
  6. Optional. Pull chicken, immersion-blend sauce smooth, return chicken.
  7. Finish (from finish tray). Stir in heavy cream + butter. Crush kasuri methi between palms, stir in off the heat. Simmer 2 min. Salt to taste. Garnish cilantro.
The smoked paprika does the work a charcoal grill would do in a restaurant — don’t omit it. If the sauce tastes flat after the IP, a splash of lemon juice or a pinch of sugar balances it.

03 — Mango Lassi

Mango Lassi

Blender 5 min Serves 4–5 Make first

Frozen + fresh mango gives you body and fresh flavor. Make it before anything else — it improves sitting in the fridge.

Tools
Blender Pitcher (for fridge) Measuring cups + spoons
Notes
  1. Make this first — only gets better sitting cold
  2. Frozen mango = body + cold; fresh mango = bright flavor. Use both.
  3. Start with 2 tbsp sugar, taste after blending. Mango ripeness varies.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups frozen mango chunks
  • 1 cup fresh mango, roughly chopped
  • 1.5 cups whole-milk yogurt
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2–3 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • pinch salt

Method

  1. Blend everything until completely smooth — frozen mango, yogurt, milk, sugar, cardamom, salt.
  2. Taste. Adjust sugar. If too thick, add milk a splash at a time.
  3. Pour into a pitcher, refrigerate until dinner. Serve cold.
