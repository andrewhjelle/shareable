Fact (the sharpest single signal)

Three separate federal IRS tax liens filed jointly against both names (2010 for $55,840.03; 2013 for $10,222.04; 2019). The 2010 lien matches the same $48,000 lien disclosed by Kofi alone in his FINRA filing (the difference is accrued interest by the time of the federal recording).

The IRS files joint liens almost exclusively against couples who filed taxes jointly (married filing jointly). Joint filing requires legal marriage in the relevant year. Rare alternative scenarios exist (joint trust liability, partnership tax liability filed personally) but are uncommon and don't typically produce repeat joint liens across multiple years.
