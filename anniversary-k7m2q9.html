Lakefront Trail
Museum Campus → Navy Pier · ~2.5 miles · leisurely

Suggested route: Start at Museum Campus (Adler / Shedd / Field). Walk north along the Lakefront Trail past Grant Park, Buckingham Fountain, Millennium Park, then all the way to Navy Pier. Skyline on your left, lake on your right. Roughly 2-3 hours strolling with stops.

Best stops:

  • Milton Lee Olive Park — quiet spot with the best skyline view. Great card-opening bench if you saved them.
  • Millennium Park — Bean photo, Crown Fountain, Lurie Garden
  • Chicago Riverwalk — detour west if you want water on both sides
  • Navy Pier — turnaround point. Ferris wheel if you're feeling it.
🚶 2-3 hrs ☀ Bring water + sunscreen 👟 Comfortable shoes

Anniversary dinner · Thattu
🍛
Thattu
Kerala Indian · Avondale · Bib Gourmand · tasting menu

Margaret Pak & Vinod Kalathil's Kerala tasting menu — small intimate room (~30 seats), chef-driven pacing. Bold South Indian flavors: pothichoru banana-leaf mains, kappa biriyani, seasonal thoran, appam. Different register than the pan-Indian you usually see in Chicago. Order the tasting if it's offered; it's a 2-hour arc that gives you natural moments to breathe.

This is the main event. Tell them it's your 20th when you sit down — they may adjust the pacing. Between courses is a natural moment for the "20 memories" ritual.
